Luke Turcotte

Health Systems Researcher

Luke Turcotte is a Health Systems Researcher working jointly with the Canadian Institute for Health Information and the University of Waterloo. His research is centred around the development and application of health information systems to support clinical decision-making and improve the quality of care in post-acute and residential long-term care settings.
